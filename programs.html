Our Story

A youth-led initiative rooted in community, driven by a vision of a digitally empowered, self-sustaining rural Uganda.

Founding Vision

Community Aid for Development was born out of a stark realization: talent is equally distributed, but opportunity is not. In the rural and underserved communities of Uganda, an entire generation of young people is being left out of the global digital economy.

We refused to wait for outside intervention. Formed by young Ugandans who have lived these challenges, Community Aid for Development is a grassroots organization dedicated to bridging the divide. We believe that empowering a young person with digital skills, agricultural knowledge, and leadership capabilities transforms not just an individual, but an entire community.

Our Mission

To equip young people in underserved communities with the digital, livelihood, and leadership skills they need to create sustainable local solutions with global impact.

Our Values

Community-Led

We don't impose solutions from the outside. We build alongside the communities we serve, ensuring ownership and sustainability.

Urgency

The digital divide widens every day. We act with the urgency required to bring marginalized youth into the modern economy.

Resilience

We operate in environments where resources are scarce. We find a way to make it work, leveraging innovation and local knowledge.

Transparency

Every resource entrusted to us is a trust we take seriously. We operate with complete openness about our challenges and our impact.
