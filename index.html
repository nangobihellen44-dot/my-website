Community Aid for Development Initiative

Empowering Youth Through Digital Skills and Community Innovation

Support Our Work Our Programs

This isn't charity.
It's Community Power.

Community Aid for Development is a youth-led initiative operating where government fails and global resources rarely reach. We are building computer labs, training youth in coding and digital literacy, planting seeds for local food security, and connecting rural Uganda to global opportunity.

Our Pillars

Digital Skills

Building computer labs and training youth in coding, digital literacy, and modern software development to bridge the global opportunity gap.

Learn More

Youth Livelihood

Fostering innovation in agriculture and local enterprise, planting seeds for food security, and creating sustainable economic independence.

Learn More

Global Engagement

Empowering young leaders to participate in global dialogues, driving community-led solutions to pressing systemic challenges.

Learn More

Who We Serve

Our focus is unyielding: young people aged 15–35 from rural and underserved communities across Uganda.

1

Rural Youth

Disconnected from major urban centers, lacking basic digital infrastructure and formal employment opportunities.

2

Young Women

Historically excluded from tech and leadership, we prioritize programs that bring women into the digital economy.

3

Local Innovators

Entrepreneurs and farmers looking for modern tools and knowledge to scale their impact locally.
