The Human Side of Impact

Behind Every Number
Is a Life Changed

Statistics tell part of the story. But real impact is the 19-year-old in Karamoja who opened a laptop for the first time and wrote her first line of code. It's the farmer who doubled his yield using techniques from our livelihood program. It's the young woman who now speaks at district council meetings.

Every resource we raise, every lab we build, every training we run is accountable to these faces — the youth of rural Uganda who deserve a fair shot at opportunity.
