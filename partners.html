Partner With Us

This work requires a coalition. Join us in equipping the next generation of Ugandan leaders.

Why Partner with Community Aid for Development?

We are deeply embedded in the communities we serve. When you partner with us, your resources bypass bureaucratic friction and go directly to frontline impact. We offer transparency, measurable outcomes, and a relentless drive to scale what works.

Foundations

Fund scalable models for rural digital literacy and agricultural resilience.

Tech Companies

Provide hardware, software licenses, or mentorship for our coding bootcamps.

Individuals

Sponsor a student, fund a computer lab, or provide monthly operational support.

Ready to initiate a partnership?

We are actively seeking strategic partners for 2025 to expand our digital skills hubs into three new districts.
